Comic Book Quiz Game

This is a multiple choice quiz game based on DC and Marvel comic books, you will have ten seconds to answer each question, there will be 8 questions that get progressively harder, at the end of the game your score will be displayed and you will have the opportunity to add your score to the highscore list if you wish to do so.
